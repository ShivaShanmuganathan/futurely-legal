Privacy Policy for Futurely

Last Updated: October 30, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Futurely ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data in an open and transparent manner. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

Key Points:
  • We collect photos and personal data to create AI-generated manifestation images
  • Your data is used solely for app functionality - we never sell your data
  • You can delete your account and data at any time
  • We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information You Provide

  • Apple Sign-In Data: When you sign in with Apple, we may receive your name and email address (if you choose to share them)
  • Profile Information: Name, age, birth date, gender, and life phase information you provide during onboarding
  • Goals and Preferences: Your manifestation goals, focus areas, preferred visualization styles, and personalization preferences
  • Photos: Selfies and photos you upload to personalize your AI-generated images

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, app version
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the App, features you use, and app performance data
  • Generated Images: AI-generated manifestation images created for you

2.3 Payment Information

  • Subscription Data: Managed through Apple's In-App Purchase system and RevenueCat
  • We do not directly handle or store your credit card information
  • Apple processes all payments according to their privacy policy

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • AI Image Generation: Create personalized manifestation images using your photos and preferences
  • App Functionality: Provide core features including daily image delivery, notifications, and personalized content
  • Account Management: Authenticate your account, manage your subscription, and sync data across devices
  • Improvements: Analyze usage patterns to improve app performance and user experience
  • Communications: Send you push notifications about your daily images (you can opt-out anytime)
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with applicable laws and respond to legal requests

4. AI and Image Processing

Our App uses artificial intelligence to generate personalized visualization images:

  • Third-Party AI Services: We use OpenAI, Google Generative AI, and other AI providers to generate images
  • Photo Processing: Your uploaded photos are processed to create AI-generated manifestation images
  • Data Sharing: Your photos may be sent to third-party AI services for processing, but they are not used to train AI models
  • Image Storage: Generated images are stored securely to enable viewing and sharing within the App

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

5.1 Service Providers

  • AI Services: OpenAI, Google Generative AI for image generation
  • Subscription Management: RevenueCat for managing subscription status
  • Push Notifications: Apple Push Notification service for sending daily reminders
  • Authentication: Apple Sign-In for secure authentication

5.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, subpoena, or other legal process, or if we believe disclosure is necessary to:

  • Comply with applicable laws or regulations
  • Protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property
  • Enforce our terms of service

6. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: We retain your data as long as your account is active and you use the App
  • Subscription Data: Payment and subscription history is retained for legal and accounting purposes
  • Deleted Accounts: When you delete your account, we delete your personal data within 30 days, except where required by law

7. Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update or correct your personal information in the App settings
  • Deletion: Delete your account and personal data at any time through App settings
  • Opt-Out: Disable push notifications or specific data collection features
  • Export: Request an export of your data in a machine-readable format

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@manifest-app.com.

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication via Apple Sign-In
  • Regular security assessments
  • Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis

Note: No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have data protection laws different from those in your country. By using the App, you consent to the transfer of your information to these countries.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the App
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Sending you a push notification (for significant changes)

Your continued use of the App after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Third-Party Services

Our App integrates with third-party services that have their own privacy policies:

13. California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to know whether personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell your data)
  • Right to request deletion of personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

14. European Union (GDPR) Rights

If you are in the European Union, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time
